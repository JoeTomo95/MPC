Welcome to the Modern Pie Company

The Modern Pie Company was conceptualised in a dorm room in the Reading University. Now we are crafting pies to our customers needs. The pies that we create are all handmade with locally sourced ingredients.

Breakfast

Pies and toasted sandwiches on offer. Enjoy with a steaming hot coffee created by our expert baristas.

Lunch

Light and healthy options. Pop in with the family and spread the joy with a kiddies meal. We have a wide range of lunch items on offer, grab a loyalty card and get your third lunch on us!
